How it works

Gregory, originally trained as a classical artist, now creates custom organic, all white cakes with intricate floral design for all occasions.

Wether it's a wedding, a birthday or anything in-between, we're here to make your occasion special.

Simply speak to Gregory or email him regarding time, price and customization options.
Every occasion is different.

After you're consultation, the cake will be delivered to you're occasion at your convenience--no more stress.
Payments can be made over the phone or via PayPal email.

It's as simple as it gets.
